About The Photographer

Hi, I’m Ire. For over 4 years, I’ve been capturing people and their biggest moments in a way that feels natural, clean, and real. Whether it’s a game, a portrait session, or a special event, my goal is to make you feel comfortable so your photos come out authentic and true to you.

My style focuses on natural light, simple direction, and real moments, giving you images that are both sharp and meaningful. I guide you just enough to get the best shots while keeping everything relaxed and enjoyable.

Based in the DMV and available for travel, I provide 48-hour sneak peeks so you don’t have to wait to see your photos. Your full gallery is delivered through a private online link, making it easy to view, download, and share.

If you’re looking for high-quality photos and a smooth, stress-free experience, I’ve got you covered.
